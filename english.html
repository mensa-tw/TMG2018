2018 MTAG Introduction

Mensa is the largest and oldest high IQ society in the world. Roland Berrill, an Australian barrister, and Dr. Lancelot Ware, a British scientist and lawyer, founded Mensa at Lincoln College, in Oxford, England, in 1946.

The once in a year annual gathering of Mensa Taiwan is in September, a banquet for members to chat and exchange knowledge with each other. We sincerely invite every member and your family to participate in the annual gathering, that’s why we've strived the best price with the five-star hotel Taoyuan Orchard Park Hotel for you, and hope that members would cherish and enjoy the exclusive privileges. Everyone who participates in Mensa would have the opportunity to meet all kinds of talent people from many different countries, and join many interesting activities, every person you meet in here is irreplaceable, that’s why we are Mensan.

The annual gathering this year, we’ve added many different activities and trips, invite the master-level members to give us speeches, sharing their outstanding life with experiences to broaden our horizons, moreover, making members to have a better, more amazing and abundant life.

Within the hard work and brainstorming of our members, we represent the spirit of Mensa together, we sincerely invite all Mensan to join this grand and plentiful Mensa Taiwan Annual gathering with your passion.
